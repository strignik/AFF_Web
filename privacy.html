Privacy Policy for AffirmX

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AffirmX ("we," "our," or "us") handles your information. We do not collect, store, or process personal data on our own servers. We use third-party services for analytics, attribution, and advertising; those services may collect certain data as described below. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Data We Do Not Collect on Our Servers

  • We do not collect or store personal data (such as name, email, or contact information) on our servers.
  • App usage and preferences are stored locally on your device where possible.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. No Personal Information Requested

  • You are not required to provide any personal information to use AffirmX.
  • We do not request, store, or process any personal identifiers, contact information, or sensitive data.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not have access to your data and do not transmit any data to our servers.

5. Analytics and Attribution (AppsFlyer)

We use AppsFlyer (provided by AppsFlyer Ltd.) for analytics, attribution, and to measure the effectiveness of our marketing. This helps us understand how users find and use our app and improve our services.

  • Data collected: AppsFlyer may collect device and app information such as device type, operating system, app version, install and launch events, in-app events (e.g., subscription-related events), and a pseudonymous identifier. We do not send personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name or email to AppsFlyer.
  • Tracking (IDFA): With your consent, we may request permission to track your activity across apps and websites (Apple’s App Tracking Transparency). If you allow tracking, AppsFlyer may use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for attribution and analytics. If you decline, we will not use the IDFA for tracking; analytics will still work using other non-identifying data.
  • Your choices: You can change your tracking preference at any time in your device Settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • AppsFlyer’s privacy policy: https://www.appsflyer.com/legal/privacy-policy/

6. Other Third-Party Services

  • Advertising: We may show ads in the app. Our ad partners may collect device and usage data to deliver and measure ads. We do not sell your personal information to advertisers.
  • Purchases and subscriptions: Handled by Apple’s App Store and subject to Apple’s privacy policies.
  • AI-generated content: Processed through secure, privacy-compliant services that do not store your personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the App or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when changes are made.

9. Contact Us

admin@affirmx.app

10. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Apple's App Store Guidelines
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations
